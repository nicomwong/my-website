Insecurities

How to resolve (all) insecurity

Ramp-up/onboarding ~ 3-6 mo.

  1. Try some meditation practices out. Pick a few you like and keep experimenting with them. Meditation Practices
  2. Become more and more aware of the subtler and subtler feelings and mental objects. \Start with feelings & emotional reactions and their in-the-moment connection to bodily tension. \Continue to practice being aware of every thing you can be in your experience.

Life-long process

Repeat the following process over and over in moderation across a long time span

  1. - Now, you have "perfect awareness".
  2. Resume living daily life as you had been before starting meditation.
  3. Notice what happens in your experience.
  4. Pay attention to the bubbles that come up, then they just disappear
  5. These are samskaras (of differing intensities) being released/let go/extinguished
